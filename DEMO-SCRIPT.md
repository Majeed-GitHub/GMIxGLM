### Q&A PREP

**Q: How is this different from ChatGPT/Claude?**
> ChatGPT gives you generic code. PainForge does agentic multi-step reasoning specifically
> for Indian problems — it understands GST filing, UPI reconciliation, WhatsApp Business API,
> and designs for low-bandwidth, vernacular-first, offline-capable deployment.

**Q: How does the physics work?**
> Spring-based rigid body simulation with Euler integration. The card tracks mouse velocity
> during drag, applies momentum on release, and springs back with configurable damping.
> The wind slider adds sinusoidal force on all three rotation axes.

**Q: Can this actually deploy?**
> Yes. The generated code is real Next.js + TypeScript with Prisma ORM. The deploy section
> gives step-by-step GMI Cloud CLI commands. One `gmi deploy` and it's live.

**Q: What's the business model?**
> Freemium: free tier for 3 pain forges/month. Pro at ₹999/month for unlimited. Enterprise
> for agencies at ₹4,999/month with custom integrations. Target: 10,000 paying users in 12 months.
